Our First Community Bake Sale 💗

A evening of treats, gratitude, and giving back

We are so excited to share that our very first Community Bake Sale was a wonderful success!

Throughout the day, so many people came out to support us and purchase our baked goods. We are so grateful to everyone who volunteered time, shared the event, and chose to support our mission..

Proceeds from this event went directly toward local initiatives that uplift women. Thank you to everyone who supported us and made this event possible!
